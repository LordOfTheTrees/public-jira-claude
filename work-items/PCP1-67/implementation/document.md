# Dimensional Mystic - Occult Teleporter Build

## Character Concept
A Psychic spellcaster who has mastered the art of dimensional travel through the power of the mind.

## Base Statistics

- Discipline: Self-Perfection
- Ability Scores (Point Buy):

  - DEX: 14
  - CON: 12
  - INT: 18
  - WIS: 12
  - CHA: 14
